✦ Platform Features

Everything you need in one place

Whether you're a manufacturer looking to expand your reach or an industry buyer hunting for niche components — PartLink has you covered end-to-end.

🏭

Supplier Marketplace

Register as a supplier, get approved by admins, and list your products with real-time stock tracking. Receive orders directly with full buyer contact details.

🛒

Smart Order Tracking

Buyers place orders, track live status updates — from Pending → Accepted → Shipped → Delivered. Automatic email + in-app notifications at every step.

⚙️

Admin Control Centre

Platform administrators get a full command centre — approve suppliers, broadcast notifications, update order statuses, and monitor every transaction in real time.

🔒

Role-based Security

Three distinct roles — Admin, Supplier, Buyer — each with their own protected dashboard and access controls. Suppliers require admin approval before going live.

📧

Instant Notifications

Admins can notify individual users or broadcast to all buyers/suppliers at once. Every key event in your order cycle triggers an in-app notification visible on your dashboard.

📦

Inventory Management

Suppliers manage product listings, pricing-per-unit, minimum order quantities, and stock levels. Low-stock warnings appear automatically on the supplier dashboard.
